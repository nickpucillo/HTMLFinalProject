What is Open Systems Interconnection (OSI)?

OSI is a model that describes seven layers how computer systems
communicate over a network. There are seven layers in total and
they have different responsiblies. Each layer interacts with the layer above
and below it to send data to each other.The OSI -
is very important because it is how computers and networks communicate
with each other,makes troubleshooting easier, and provide reliable networks.

7 Application 6 Presentation 5 Session 4 Transport 3 Network 2 Data Link 1 Physical
